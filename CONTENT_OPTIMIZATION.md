## Optimized Meta Tag Templates

### Title Tag Formula
```
[Service] [City] | [Benefit] | Odin Digital
```
**Character Target**: 55-60 characters

### Meta Description Formula
```
[Pain Point Solution]. [Service] for [City] businesses. [Key Metrics]. [CTA].
```
**Character Target**: 150-160 characters
