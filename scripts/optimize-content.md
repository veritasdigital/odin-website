## Image Alt Text Standards

### Location Images
- Format: `[City] skyline - [Service] services by Odin Digital`
- Example: `Melbourne skyline - SEO services by Odin Digital`

### Service Icons
- Format: `[Service] icon representing [benefit]`
- Example: `Google Ads icon representing PPC management`

### Team Photos
- Format: `[Name], [Title] at Odin Digital`
- Example: `Lucas Durante, CEO at Odin Digital`

### Client Logos
- Format: `[Client Name] logo - Odin Digital client`
- Example: `YCL Jewels logo - Odin Digital client`

## H1 Verification Checklist

✅ Single H1 per page
✅ H1 contains primary keyword only
✅ H1 in hero section (top of page)
✅ No additional text in H1
✅ Proper hierarchy: H1 → H2 → H3

## Content Length Requirements

Minimum 500+ words unique content per location page via:
- LocationContent component (local statistics, case study, landmarks)
- Service-specific content (minimum 300 words)
- FAQ section (contributes to total word count)
